Lake Menomin

Lake Menomin is located in Dunn County, Wisconsin adjacent to the city of Menomonie with a total area of approximately 1000 acres.1 Lake Menomin received its name from Senator James Huff Stout in approximately 19022

References

1."Lake Menomin". Wisconsin Departmentof Natural Resources. https://dnr.wi.gov/lakes/lakepages/LakeDetail.aspx?wbic=2065900. ↩ 2."Lake Menomin". Menomonie - LocalWiki. https://localwiki.org/menomonie/Lake_Menomin. ↩

Lake Facts/h2>
  • Area: 1009 acres
  • Max Depth: 34 feet
  • Elevation: 814 feet
  • Bottom: 70% sand, 10% gravel, 20% muck
  • Health: hypereutrophic
